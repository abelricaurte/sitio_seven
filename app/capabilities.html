Software Development

Differentiating from other offshoring and staff augmentation companies using traditional technologies, in S4N we integrate our front-end and back-end expertise in order to create exceptional experiences for our products users. Sometimes our users are final customers or employees who trust the precision and agility that our products deliver to keep ahead of the competiton. Our software development services include:

  1. Web / CMS & Transactional Services
  2. Mobile / Native, Hybrid & Responsive
  3. Enterprise / Fast Data & Reactive & Legacy
  4. Integration / Web API, Microservices

  1. Architectural definition
  2. DDD
  3. Performance enhancing & Reenginering

Training

The strength developed by the S4N team brings the capacity to transfer specialized knowledge in technologies as well as in design, architecture and methodological best practices to tackle specific technical problems in every organization.

Software Maintenance

Our vision is to partner with our customer to guarantee they can count with us along all the life cycle in every software product we published or deploy into production. Cloud Managed Services

Cloud is the new normal, offering operation efficiency as well as costs and risks reduction well beyond feasibility in traditional data centers. S4N deploys, migrates and operates the applications we craft so our customers can obtain the most solid solution from every perspective – technical, financial, compliance – in a single place.

Support

Usually combined with the cloud managed services offering, S4N also offers a 24x7 support service for every application we build and deploy on the most important PaaS and IaaS providers, like AWS, Azure and Google App Engine. This way, S4n customers have peace of mind, as they know we are looking after their business even if there is an anomaly that can risk the business operation.
